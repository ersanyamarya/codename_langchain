 To kick start a career in IoT, aspirants need to have hands-on knowledge of the following concepts: Software development, Data Management, Data Analytics, Networking Systems, Security Standards and Artificial intelligence. To start with, reach out to organisations in the area of IoT for internship programs. Stay connected with a network and thought leaders in the area and add some relevant professional certifications to your profile to add credibility. Technology wise, IoT is divided into 4 primary sections: Telecommunications-based technologies, Embedded Systems, Programming languages, and hardware like sensors and sim modules.
